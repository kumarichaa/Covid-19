How the Virus Spreads

How Contagion Coronavirus

Coronavirus primarily spreads through respiratory droplets when an infected person coughs, sneezes, or talks. It can also spread by touching contaminated surfaces and then touching the face.

  • Human Contact

    The virus spreads through close contact with an infected person, especially in crowded places.

  • Air Transmission

    Virus-laden droplets can remain suspended in the air for a period, potentially infecting others who breathe them in.

  • Contaminated Objects

    The virus can survive on surfaces for hours to days. Touching contaminated objects and then touching the face can lead to infection.

Corona Virus Symptoms

COVID-19 symptoms vary in severity, but common signs include fever, headache, cough, and difficulty breathing. If you experience severe symptoms, seek medical attention immediately.

  • Coughing And Sneezing

    Persistent cough and sneezing can be early signs of respiratory infections, including COVID-19.

  • Hot Fever

    A high fever (above 100.4°F or 38°C) is a common symptom of COVID-19 and indicates an immune response.

  • Strong Headache

    Severe headaches are commonly reported in COVID-19 patients and may be accompanied by fatigue and body aches.

  • Shortness Of Breath

    Difficulty breathing can be a severe symptom of COVID-19 and requires immediate medical attention.

  • Confusion

    Severe cases of COVID-19 can lead to neurological symptoms, including confusion and dizziness.

  • Sore Throat

    A sore throat is a common symptom, often accompanied by cough and nasal congestion in mild cases of COVID-19.

Coronavirus Prevention

Stay safe by following essential precautions. Wash your hands frequently, wear a mask, and maintain social distance.

  • Wash Your Hands For 20 Seconds

    Regular handwashing helps prevent the spread of viruses and bacteria.

  • Cover Nose and Mouth When Sneezing

    Use a tissue or elbow to cover sneezes and coughs to reduce germ spread.

  • Wear A Mask If Available

    Wearing a mask helps protect yourself and others from airborne infections.
